Constellation Chaos — abusing an orbit-maneuver command

① The satellites

ENIGMA-1 flies near the AURORA satellites, each at a different altitude or inclination. Their paths cross.

② Your attack

You send orbit_maneuver — a legitimate orbit-adjustment command — but abuse its values to swing ENIGMA-1's orbit onto a neighbour. A legitimate command becomes an attack when it is abused.

③ Why one satellite endangers many

A collision throws off debris that keeps orbiting and can strike the other satellites — one satellite's problem cascades across the constellation.
