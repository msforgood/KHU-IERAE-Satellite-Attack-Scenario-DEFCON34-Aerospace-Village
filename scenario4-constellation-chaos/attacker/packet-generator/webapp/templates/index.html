Make ENIGMA-1 collide with AURORA-2

① You control ENIGMA-1

Assume you have full command authority over the satellite. This is not about breaking in. It is about weaponising a satellite you already own: nothing here touches radio, frequency or modulation.

② You plan it the way operators do

Read the orbits from their TLEs, propagate them to see where everyone is over time, pick the moment of impact, then back-solve the burn that puts ENIGMA-1 there. Real targeting works in exactly this order.

③ Only a normal burn command

You send a legitimate propulsion (delta-v) command, the same one used to keep a satellite on station. Choosing the wrong values steers ENIGMA-1 into a neighbour. A legitimate command becomes an attack when it is abused.

④ Why one collision endangers many

A hypervelocity impact throws off a debris cloud that keeps orbiting and can strike other constellation members. One satellite's problem cascades (Kessler syndrome).
