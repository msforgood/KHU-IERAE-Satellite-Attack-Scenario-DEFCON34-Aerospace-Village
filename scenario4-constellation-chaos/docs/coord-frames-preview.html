Key bridge: inertial frame ↔ Earth-fixed frame are linked by a rotation of sidereal time (the Earth rotation angle). RTN is pulled directly from the inertial state vector, and this is where our scenario's Δv breakdown (along-track, out of plane) and collision distance are computed. In the demo the gray paths (Earth-fixed / geodetic / topocentric) are omitted and the Earth is treated as decoration.
